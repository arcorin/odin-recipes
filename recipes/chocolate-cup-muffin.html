Chocolate Cup Muffin

Original Recipe:

Easy, Fast Chocolate Cup Muffin

allrecipes.com

Chocolate Cup Muffin's Photo from allrecipes.com

Description

This muffin in a cup is a super-easy snack that can be made in the first five minutes after you get up. You just have to be sure to melt the butter beforehand.

  • Prep Time

    5 mins

  • Cook Time

    5 mins

  • Total Time

    10 mins

  • Servings

    1

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 ½ tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter
  • 1 tablespoon baking cocoa
  • ½ tablespoon white sugar

Steps

  1. Combine egg, flour, melted butter, cocoa, and sugar in a large microwave-safe cup and thoroughly mix. Cook in the microwave for about 2 minutes.
